FAQ's

What is the purpose of the Backend API?

The Backend API is designed to help businesses track their CO2 emissions and improve sustainability practices.

How do I use the API?

Simply enter the required start and end dates, select your facility, and submit the form to receive emission data.

Can I add multiple facilities at once?

Yes, you can add multiple facilities by clicking the "Add" button next to each one you wish to select.
